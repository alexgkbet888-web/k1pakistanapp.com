K1 official account access guide

Why can I not log in to K1?

Common causes include a wrong phone number format, expired OTP, old SIM access, a changed password, account lock messages, or a login route that does not match the registered account.

What should I do if the OTP is not received?

Check the SIM signal, SMS storage, correct country or local number format, resend timing, and whether the OTP went to an old number connected to the account.

How should I reset a K1 login password?

Use the password reset flow connected to the registered phone number. Do not give your password or OTP to a public agent or social media contact.

Why does K1 say account locked or session expired?

The message may appear after too many failed attempts, device changes, stale sessions, or app cache issues. Wait before retrying and use the support path if the same error repeats.

Is app login different from web login?

Yes. The app may require device permission, app version checks, or OTP timing, while web login usually depends more on browser cache, URL path, and session state.
