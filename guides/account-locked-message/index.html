FAQ

Does account locked mean banned?

Not necessarily. It can also mean security hold, verification requirement, or repeated failed login attempts.

Should I keep trying passwords?

No. Repeated attempts can make the lock harder to resolve.

What evidence is useful?

Message, time, device, login method, and the last successful access date.
